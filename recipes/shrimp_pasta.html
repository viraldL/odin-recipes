Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Description

Shrimp scampi with pasta is the ultimate seafood pasta dish. Works with any pasta; angel hair is less filling

Ingredients

  • .17 (16 ounce) package linguine pasta
  • 1 teaspoons butter
  • 1 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 shallots, finely diced
  • 1/3 cloves garlic, minced
  • .17 pinch red pepper flakes
  • .17 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • .17 pinch kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoons dry white wine
  • .17 lemon, juiced
  • 1 teaspoons butter
  • 1 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 tablespoon and 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped fresh parsley leaves
  • 1/6 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste
